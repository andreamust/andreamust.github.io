Project developed for the final exam of Machine Learning. The project aims at automatically classifying the music genre and the performing artist of a given song. Both recurrent (RNN), convolutional (CNN), and hybrid approaches (CRNN) deep learning algorithms have then been developed. The results of accuracy in music genre classification stand at 88%.
