Project developed for the final examination in Knowledge Organization and Digital Methods in the Cultural Heritage Domain. ApoLOD11 project creates a Linked Open Data environment for key concepts, figures, and items surrounding the idea of the first lunar exploration, representing the relationships through an E/R Model. The end result is representative items linked in a LOD manner that follows industry standards.
