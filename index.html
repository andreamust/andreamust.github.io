Assistant Researcher

Andrea Poltronieri

I am an assistant researcher at LILEC department at Bologna University.
I have a M.A. in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge from the University of Bologna (2021), and a B.A. in Drama, Arts, and Music Studies from the University of Bologna (2018).
My research interests include Knowledge Representation and Extraction, Music Information Retrieval, Machine Learning and Computational Musicology.
I am currently a contributor of the project ArCo4Science (MiBACT).
